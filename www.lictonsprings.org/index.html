Welcome!

Licton Springs is a beautiful neighborhood in North Seattle (view map), bounded by Interstate 5 to the east, Highway 99 to the west, N. 85th St. to the south, and N. 105th (Northgate Way) to the north. Read about the history of the area on the Local Interest page.
