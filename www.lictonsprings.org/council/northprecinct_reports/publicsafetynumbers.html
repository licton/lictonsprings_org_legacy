NORTH PRECINCT PUBLIC SAFETY NUMBERS

Police Department Resource Numbers Call 9-1-1 (24 hours) for: Emergency; all medical, fire and police emergencies, and anytime you believe there is a need to send police, fire or medical personnel to the scene. 911 calls are free from payphones

Call 625-5011 (24 hours) for Non Emergency: Any incident which you believe should be reported to the Police Department but does not require an immediate dispatch of an officer to the scene.

Call the North Precinct Watch Commander (24 hrs.) at 684-0856: If you have an administrative or procedural question, or if you have a comment regarding an officer’s actions, or a Thank You, or if you have a suggestion.

The North Precinct address is 10049 College Way North, Seattle, Washington 98133.

Call the North Precinct Community Policing Team at 684-0794: If you have a chronic, on-going problem that has not been resolved by calling 9-1-1.

Call the NarcoticsNice at 684-5797 for: On-going suspected drug activity (8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.).
